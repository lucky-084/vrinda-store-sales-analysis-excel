# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis (Microsoft Excel)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end sales analysis of **Vrinda Store's Annual Sales Data (2022)** using **Microsoft Excel**. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify sales trends, analyze customer behavior, and support data-driven decision-making.

The project demonstrates practical data analytics skills, including data cleaning, data transformation, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI reporting, and dashboard design.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The primary objective of this analysis is to answer key business questions such as:

- Which months generated the highest sales?
- What is the relationship between sales and order volume?
- Which sales channels contribute the most revenue?
- Which states generate the highest sales?
- What is the customer distribution by gender and age group?
- What percentage of orders were delivered, cancelled, returned, or refunded?

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance

- Sales remained strongest during the first quarter, with peak revenue recorded in **March**.
- Sales gradually declined during the second half of the year.

### Customer Demographics

- **Women contributed approximately 64% of total sales**, indicating a significantly larger customer base than men.
- Adult customers represented the highest purchasing segment across both genders.

### Order Status

- Approximately **92% of orders were successfully delivered**, indicating strong operational performance.
- Returns, cancellations, and refunds collectively accounted for only a small percentage of total orders.

### Top Performing States

The highest revenue-generating states were:

1. Maharashtra
2. Karnataka
3. Uttar Pradesh
4. Telangana
5. Tamil Nadu

### Sales Channels

The majority of sales were generated through:

- Amazon
- Myntra
- Flipkart

These three platforms accounted for the largest share of customer orders.
